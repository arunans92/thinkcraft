About us

Thinkcraft Automation Pvt Ltd is a 2024 startup and a micro enterprise as on date. Formation of 'Thinkcraft Automation' was aimed to offer innovative solutions for both civil and military applications. Accordingly, a set of professionals from different engineering fields patched up and formed the company with Indian Government vision of achieving self-reliance in technology. The startup is currently involved in R & D of technology products and developing prototypes through innovative solutions with cost effectiveness.
