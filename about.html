About us

Thinkcraft Pvt Ltd is a 2024 startup and a micro enterprise as on date. The company was split up from Tesla Projects, Sankagiri which was established in year 2021 and dealt with manufacturing of consumer electronics products and goods. The split up and formation of 'Thinkcraft' was aimed to strategically revamp the operations. A set of experienced professionals with varied field experience patched up with a vision of achieving self reliance in military and civil technologies.
